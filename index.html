What is a Bully Stick?

A bully stick is a long-lasting, single-ingredient dog treat made from the pizzle, or penis, of a bull or steer. These treats are high in protein and low in fat, making them a healthy and delicious option for your furry friend. Bully sticks are also great for dental health, helping to scrape away plaque and tartar as your dog chews. They are a natural alternative to traditional rawhide chews and are often preferred by pet parents who want to avoid artificial ingredients.

What is a Yak Chew?

A yak chew is a long-lasting, all-natural dog treat made from the milk of yaks or cows found in the Himalayan region. The milk is processed into a hard cheese that is then smoked and cured into chewable sticks or bars. Yak chews are a high-protein, low-fat, and easily digestible treat that can help to promote dental health and reduce stress and anxiety in dogs. They are often referred to as "Himalayan chews" or "Himalayan dog chews." Yak chews have become a popular alternative to traditional rawhide chews due to their natural composition and long-lasting durability.
